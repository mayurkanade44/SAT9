CQR
Contract QR

Contract-QR is MERN stack web app, used by EPCORN to create a pest control contract and service cards for clients. Each service card has unique QR code, which helps service operator to quickly & easily update the status after the service.

CBDP
Cloud Based Document Portal

CBDP is Cloud Based Documents Portal is MERN app. It is used by EPCORN for sending MSDS, TC & SOP documents of various pest services to clients with inbuilt emailing function. Also has Upskill section to provide training and information videos to operators.
