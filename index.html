CQR
Contract QR

Contract-QR is MERN stack web app, used by EPCORN to create a pest control contract and service cards for clients. Each service card has unique QR code, which helps service operator to quickly & easily update the status after the service.

CBDP
Cloud Based Document Portal

CBDP is Cloud Based Document repository Portal, developed by EPCORN for quicker and easier access of documents needed by teams and customers, like MSDS, TC & SOP documents of various pest services having an inbuilt emailing function. We have added “UPSKILL” a section to provide quick glimpse providing training and information videos to stake holders.

Reports Generation
Reports Generation

Reports Generation portal is used to crate RIM/Work Done/Inspection and many report automatically. Here user has to select predefined template and upload images with appropriate comments. And back office person will get the generated file, which he/she can edit/send to client with in-build email functionality.

SAI
System Assisted Intelligence

At EPCORN our core value of ensuring we provide the best and dependable services without compromising on chemicals and safety is prime. To ensure we at a team always have the right approach developed SAI, to embolden the decision taking of our teams in handling pests and deploying right strategy and chemicals for the various pest problems in experienced by our teams. Our aim was to pool in data over the years to provide the right past reference fitting into the algorithm so as to blend in artificial intelligence and predictive guidance based on known parameters needed for decision taking in the pest control industry.
